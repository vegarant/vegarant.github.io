Details about the courses

MAT-INF 1100 and MAT-IN 1105 (previously MAT-INF 1100L) are first semester courses at the University of Oslo with around 300 and 200 students, respectively. The two courses have separate teaching in the first half of the semester and joint teaching the second half. I first started working at a teaching assistant (TA) in the course MAT-INF 1100 in 2012. Since then, I've been a TA in this course and the courses MAT-INF 1100L / MAT-IN 1105 several times. From 2016 to 2018, I gave the plenary lectures in MAT-INF 1100, solving the weekly exercise in front of the students. The autumn of 2019, I was lecturing MAT-INF 1100 together with Knut Mørken.

I have worked as a teaching assistant in the courses MAT-INF 2360 and MAT 3110/4110, in 2017 and 2020, respectively.
